In Plain Language

What do we actually mean by that?

LMV will become the daily home for civic life.

A place where you get information you can actually trust. A place where you build real skills: speech and debate, media training, board readiness. A platform where your community has a standing seat at the table, not a one-time invitation. And a platform where your voice becomes policy, not just a comment card.

Our Next Evolution

From civic access to civic leadership

Founded in 2007 by a multiracial coalition of voters, LMV has spent nearly twenty years earning trust: listening, educating, convening, and serving communities year after year.

That trust is now fueling something bigger. Community voice shapes what we communicate. What we communicate develops new leaders. Those leaders engage their communities, and that engagement sparks the next round of priorities. Then the loop starts again.

How We Get There

Build. Develop. Evolve.

Right now
Build

We're restructuring governance from an all-volunteer model into a hybrid of paid staff and volunteer leadership. Aligning our mission and vision, and turning two decades of local trust into a shared knowledge base and reporting system across every chapter. This is the foundation everything else stands on.

Next 12 to 24 months
Develop

We're standing up the content engine itself: paid communications staff, cross-platform strategy, and the infrastructure to reach citizens on the platforms they already use. Bringing on our first paid national staff and state directors, opening a DC presence, and relaunching the bipartisan convenings that make LMV different from anyone else in this work.

The next twenty years
Evolve

We become what doesn't exist yet: a national, always-on civic participation platform. Millions of people informed, developed as leaders, engaged in their communities, and represented in the rooms where decisions get made. Every day of the year, not once every four.

Our Vision

 

A democracy where minority and underrepresented communities aren't just included. They lead. They're self-determining, informed, and represented at every table. Parity that's real, not promised, and civic power that moves all year long, not once every four years.
How It Works

One always-on content engine.

Fueling four connected platforms, on a constant loop.

The Model

Four platforms, always connected

01 Inform
The always-on content engine

A cross-platform content strategy that meets citizens where they already are: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, podcasts, not just our own channels. Real-time issue explainers, candidate explainers & community data, adapted for whichever platform people actually use. Fuels every other platform.

02 Develop
Leadership pipeline

Speech, debate & civic literacy training. Media training that teaches leaders to create content on the same platforms Inform runs. Candidate & board readiness, built for reps, not theory.

03 Engage
Engagement network

Year-round chapters, local and digital. Forums, listening sessions, local partnerships. Where community input actually goes somewhere.

04 Represent
Voice-to-power loop

Policy research & community surveys. Legislative engagement & public recommendations. Feeds directly back into Inform & Develop.
